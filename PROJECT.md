# Multibagger

## Vision

Multibagger is a modular Python-based momentum investing research platform focused on Indian equities.

The objective is to identify high-quality growth stocks using systematic, rules-based analysis inspired by investors and traders such as:

- Stan Weinstein
- Mark Minervini
- William O'Neil
- Stockbee (Pradeep Bonde)
- Nicolas Darvas

---

## Mission

Provide traders and investors with an end-to-end research platform capable of:

- Downloading market data
- Computing technical indicators
- Running multiple scanners
- Ranking investment opportunities
- Backtesting strategies
- Producing professional reports

---

## Core Principles

- Modular Architecture
- Single Responsibility Principle
- Clean Code
- Testable Components
- Reusable Indicator Engine
- Rules-Based Decision Making
